# Augmentor

**GitHub:** https://github.com/seung-lab/Augmentor
**Language:** Jupyter Notebook | **Stars:** 1

Data augmentation toolbox for training 3D convolutional networks. Developed for the superhuman SNEMI3D connectomics challenge (Lee et al. 2017).

## Key Features
- 3D-aware data augmentation for volumetric EM data
- Designed to pair with DataProvider3

## Relevance to Connectomics
Provides 3D augmentation transforms for training EM segmentation networks, an earlier approach to what MONAI transforms now provide in PyTC.
