# FilterFinder

**GitHub:** https://github.com/seung-lab/FilterFinder
**Language:** Jupyter Notebook | **Stars:** 1

Filter Finder for Deep Normalized Cross-Correlation on EM images of neurons. Trains filters for image alignment of serial EM sections.

## Key Features
- Deep normalized cross-correlation for EM image registration
- Docker-based workflow with nvidia-docker support
- TensorBoard visualization of training progress
- Jupyter notebook for interactive evaluation

## Relevance to Connectomics
Used for EM section alignment, a critical preprocessing step before segmentation in the connectomics pipeline.
